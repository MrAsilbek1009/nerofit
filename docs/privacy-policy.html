1. Overview

Nerofit is a fitness application that provides personalized workouts, progress and nutrition tracking, and an AI coaching assistant. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who processes it, and the choices you have.

We do not intentionally collect special-category data beyond the fitness/health inputs you choose to enter, and we do not use that data for advertising.

3. How we use your data

  • Provide, personalize and improve the workouts, tracking and coaching.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • Measure usage (in aggregate) and diagnose crashes.
  • Communicate essential service messages (e.g. password reset).

We do not sell your personal data.

4. AI coaching

When you message the AI coach, your message and limited profile context (such as your goal and equipment) are sent to our AI provider to generate a response. Do not share information in the chat that you would not want processed for this purpose. The AI coach is not a medical service.

6. Retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account. This removes your account and associated data from our systems. Some records may be retained where required by law or by the app stores for transaction history.

7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Access to your records is restricted by per-user database security rules so that you can only access your own data. Privileged operations run on the server, never in the app.

8. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise deletion in-app; for other requests contact us (§11).

9. Children

Nerofit is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International transfers

Our providers may process data in countries other than yours. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.
